What

A friend once asked me to make a match-3 game. "What a stupid idea", I replied. Then a few years later I thought about it again and decided to do it anyway, but in my own way. This is what I made.

It's an interactive story and a match-3 game that takes you through life.

Why

After Barbearian, I wanted to do something more intimate and personal. Day Repeat Day is something that takes a very commercial genre (match-3) and tries to give it meaning through a story with real people and their issues, topped with a sprinkle of satire and mysteries.
