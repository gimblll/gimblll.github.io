About

A friend once asked me to make a match-3 game. "What a stupid idea", I replied. Then a few years later I thought about it again and decided to do it anyway, but in my own way. This is what I made.

It's an interactive story and a match-3 game that takes you through life.

Maybe you'll like it, maybe you won't. Who knows?
